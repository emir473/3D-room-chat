Welcome to your 3D Multimedia Space!

Connect instantly with your friends in real-time using high-end interactive systems.

How to Connect & Start

1. Copy your generated 6-digit ID on the main page dashboard.

2. Share this ID directly with your friend via any external channel.

3. Paste your friend's ID into the text field and click Connect.

4. Once established, tap Join 3D Room to activate the interactive simulation environment!

Features & Controls Guide

  • Calling System (Top-Right): Tap the phone receiver icon 📞 for a crisp Voice Call, or the camera icon 📹 for an HD Video Call. When called, a full-screen notification popup layout will allow you to Accept or Decline the session.
  • Automatic Status Update: As soon as a connection is established and the stream arrives, the calling screen immediately switches to "Call Active" and reveals your call control dashboard.
  • In-Call Camera Upgrades: Want to show your face mid-conversation? Press the camera icon 📷 inside an audio-only call to send a structured data request (camera_request) to your friend. A prompt will pop up on their screen asking to switch to a Video Call. Once accepted, both cameras initialize instantly without closing the overlay.
  • In-Call Dashboard: During an active call, you can easily toggle your camera, mute your microphone, mute your friend's incoming sound, or instantly end the session with the red Hang Up action button.
  • iPad-Style Attachment Tool (Bottom-Left): Tap the ➕ button right beside the chat box to choose between uploading generic files, browsing your media gallery, or activating your camera hardware directly to snap photos and capture videos.
  • Voice Messages (Bottom-Right): Tap or hold the microphone icon 🎙 right next to the message line to dynamically record voice notes and stream them straight into the shared text stream history as HTML5 playbacks.
